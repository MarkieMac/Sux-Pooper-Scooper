Sux Pooper Scooper is a woman owned, small family business. I created this business because I saw that there was a need for this service and wanted to help others in my community. I have a love for animals which started with dog walking and sitting to picking up the dogs waste to help their owners enjoy more quality time with their fur babies!


About me:

Hi! I am Markie McClure I am the ownner of Sux Pooper Scooper. I am a mother to two beautiful children. They are for sure my biggest push in starting my own company. I want them to know and learn that you can do anything you put your mind to. I am a Sioux City native, I graduated from Sergeant Bluff.
